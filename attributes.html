Sheffield Graduate Attributes

Sheffield Graduate Attributes are a group of skills that have been deemed helpful for university studies, employment and beyond by the university and its partners. The attributes are split into three groups: My learning, My impact and My self.

Extra Information

mySkills

mySkills is a way to record all the new transferable skills and attributes picked up during university. Once in the mySkills portfolio, these skills can be built upon as they are all in one space and simple to find.

Extra Information

My Experiences

My course has helped me to improve my approach to problems as this is an important skill for Computer Science. This attribute is a part of the My learning category.

One of the attributes in the My impact category is collaboration, which is a big part of the Computer Science course. The group projects have helped to develop this skill.

The assignments and weekly tasks set for each module have pushed me to improve my determination and planning, which is part of the My self attributes.
